Your complete guide to accessing, using, and learning about the resources and services of the National Library of India.

Accessing the Library

The National Library is open to all readers. Register and obtain a library card to enjoy our facilities and resources.

Using Resources

Explore our vast collection of books, manuscripts, periodicals, and digital archives. Our team is ready to assist you.

Rules and Guidelines

Maintain silence and adhere to library rules. Food and beverages are not permitted in reading areas.
