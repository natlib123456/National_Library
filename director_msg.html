Director General's Message

The National Library of India, foremost among the libraries in India, is one of the four designated libraries entitled to receive under the Delivery of Books and Newspapers (Public Libraries) Act, 1954 a copy of every publication published anywhere in the country. The Library is a permanent depository of all reading and printed materials produced in India, or written by any foreigner, wherever published and in whatever language.

The National Library of India offers its vast treasure of books, magazines and journals published across the country and the world to researchers/scholars as well as lay readers. Publications from across the world are housed in it and are preserved and restored for the use of the readers of today and tomorrow. Although the Covid-19 pandemic has affected the entire society, the National Library continues with its services with the pandemic protocol in place for the welfare of our readers.

In view of the current constraints on mobility, the National Library has introduced an online registration process. Any citizen beyond 18 years of age can become a member of the Library by submitting the Registration Form from anywhere within the country on the National Library website. Citizens may also reach out to the National Library through Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and Instagram.

To cope with the changing time, the Ministry of Culture, Government of India has launched the Indian Culture Portal, a National Virtual Library which showcases information about the rich tangible and intangible cultural heritage of India. The portal hosts data from archives, museums, monuments, knowledge traditions, visual and material arts, performing arts, fairs and festivals, practices and rituals etc. The National Library has also made available its digitized collection of about 15,147 books from where citizens may view and download books.

The National Library of India is committed to providing access to the tangible printed heritage of India in multiple forms and modes and strives continuously to improve its services for its readers.
